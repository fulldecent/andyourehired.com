Who's who

"We", "our", "us", "the site", "the services" refer to the And You're Hired website and Wayne Pearson, its owner and operator.

"You", "your" refer to you, dear visitor, job seeker and/or hiring manager using the site and the services.

Data collection

While using the site, you may post information such as your resume, candidate search terms or other information. Your web browser may also send information such as your IP address, browser identification or other system information. We collect and use this information to render the services.

Hiring managers

We make money by allowing hiring managers to contact people that posted a resume. If you will contact a person that posted a resume then you agree that it will be solely for the purpose of evaluating that candidate for direct hire.

Cookies

One specific type of data collection is cookies. Can can disable and delete cookies in your browser any time, just search the web and you can figure it out. We and Google Analytics use cookies on the site in order to render the services and monitor our delivery of the services.

Representations

Our website allows you to post information (resumes, contact information) so that others may search for it. We make this obvious by allowing you to see which information is public and which is private. When you post information and/or mark it as public, you grant us a non-exclusive, worldwide, paid-up, royalty free license to use that information. (Basically, you post it and then other people can see it.)

When you post information, you represent and assert that you have full authority to give it to us for this purpose. (In other words, don't post things that are protected by copyright or which you don't have permission to post!) Further, you represent and assert that all information you post is accurate.
